   (i tell you a true story, lifeexpirence

   i tell you!)

   my parents are croatian so i grew

   up speaking croatian so i went

   to a lady next to the bakery

   in gracanica a serbian enclave

   in kosova

   and there i talked to the lady fist

   i asked if she spoke croatian

   since my parents are croatienne

   and i grew up speaking croatino

   next to the other countries and

   she got angy and shocked me she

   poked me with her nose she shook

   her head since she did not speak

   croatian! but serbian!

   i was stupid. asking the lady if she

   spoke croatian since i had no idea that

   i was in gracanica 5 miles to prishtina

   a serbian enclave because i took the bus

   to the mall and got off too late

   by accident i fell asleep. so i landed

   in an enclave. how unusual!

   and i just wanted to ask

   the lady where i was

   in croat

   but all she spoke was

   another thing

on the beach for all our poems

off each fingernail-

Foreign Drama

it’s going okay i guess

 

herring is the most widely consumed fish in the world but i’ve never tried it

apparently it has great nutritional value. i haven’t fact checked any of this, aside from the fact that i haven’t tried it

humans are very impressed by things that function against what is common in nature. salmon swim upstream. the nile flows north. wow yes very impressive. i’ve never been a salmon or a river, but i’ve never really tried. being a fish or a body of water is pretty common, as far as existing on earth goes.

there are a lot of things i haven’t tried yet. pretty much all of my effort goes into trying to be happy. it’s going okay i guess. do you know of any places that serve good herring?
